Undergraduate Research Opportunities at the U of O

Research is an essential part of the undergraduate experience. The skills you'll gain by working on real problems outside the classroom are invaluable for post-graduate life.

Here, we have compiled a list of UO Professors who are actively conducting research in their fields of specialization. Don't hesitate to contact a professor if you are interested in assisting them in their research! Many are happy to accept undergraduate help.
